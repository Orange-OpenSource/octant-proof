Certifying a Datalog optimization in Coq/SSReflect

Pierre-Léo Bégay1,2 , Pierre Crégut1, and Jean-François Monin2

1 Orange
2 Verimag

Abstract. We introduce a static analysis and a program transformation for Datalog to circumvent performance issues that arise with the imple- mentation of primitive predicates, notably in the framework of a large scale telecommunication application. To this effect, we introduce a new trace semantics for Datalog with a verified mechanization. This work can be seen as both a first step and a proof of concept for the creation of a full-blown library of verified Datalog optimizations, on top of an existing Coq/SSReflect formalization of Datalog towards the development of a realistic environment for certified data-centric applications.
